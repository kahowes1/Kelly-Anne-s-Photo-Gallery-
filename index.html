I spy

I took this photo of a fall reef at a craft shop in downtown Blacksburg. I liked how the image looked like a page from an I spy book with the random smiley face on a pumpkin and the scattered pinecones.

Into the woods

Blacksburg has a lot of fun things to do outside in the fall such as hiking. I took this photo of Sierra on a trail behind my apartment to capture the outdoorsy lifestyle of those in the town.

As far as the eye can see

I took this photo of pumpkins outside of a grocery store in town. The angle I shot the photo from makes the rows of pumpkins look like they go on forever

Beautiful Day

I took this photo at the Hahn Horticulture Garden at Virginia Tech. The weather in Blacksburg is still warm and sunny in early fall and residents often spend time outside.

Nature Walk

I took this photo while on the trail behind my apartment. I really liked the way the trees curled over the path in an arch.

Changing Colors

The leaves on the trees in Blacksburg start to turn mid fall. I took this photo of a tree outside my apartment to show the beginning of the change of season.

booties

About me
